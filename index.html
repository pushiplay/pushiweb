Decentralized Evolutive Ecosystem

Cryptocurrency developed for the community, DAO, fast payments, privacy, masternodes and much more!

MasterNodes

Earn up to 45% of the block reward running a node and contributing to the network. Masternodes require 1000 PUSHI as collateral.

Instant Transactions

Secure instant transactions by the InstantSend technology. Send or receive transfers in a fraction of second.

Anonymous

PrivateSend technology adding privacy to transactions. Identical inputs, transactions cannot be directly traced, obfuscating the flow of funds and make anonymous transfers.

Governance and Funding

Decentralized Governance by Blockchain (DGBB), consensus on proposed network changes and funding development and evolution of the Pushi ecosystem.

Trades will occur directly between users (peer to peer) through an automated decentralized process.
